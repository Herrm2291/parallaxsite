Email me at jeffwmorris89@yahoo.com and in the subject PLEASE put your name.
I ask that everyone please propose their idea as clearly and specific as can be. I need approximate size, location you want it on your body, and if you’d like it in color or not.

Include reference photos. Just a few please, or whatever is needed to get your idea across, as best as possible.

Let’s share that sort of thing in person if you’d like! I would love to hear why you’re getting the tattoo, but won’t have time to read about it!

If you ask me to tattoo someone else’s tattoo on you exactly I will not accept your proposal. If you want something done by someone else go to that person!

The shop has hired help everyday to facilitate appointment bookings and receiving of down payments. We accept credit or cash. Your dowmpayment will come off the first appointment price.
